ABOUT MSW

MSW does not simply service
the commercial real estate industry, we are part of the commercial real estate industry.

Every attorney at MSW focuses solely on commercial real estate matters. Through the development of individualized, client-focused strategies, our team works tirelessly to create a blueprint for success and advance our clients' interests in every matter.

The firm's attorneys regularly represent businesses, investors, and lenders in complex real estate and commercial transactions, banking and financing matters, and real estate litigation. The firm's attorneys also routinely represent clients in connection with the procurement of planning, zoning, and other development approvals, and help clients identify, negotiate, and secure real estate and economic development incentives, such as tax credits, tax abatementsand exemptions, and other forms of non-dilutive financing.

With offices in Newark and New York City, MSW was founded to provide effective, efficient, practical and creative legal and advisory services to meet the distinctive needs of our clients. Our attorneys have broad private and public sector experience, coming from top national law firms and high-level governmental positions. Through the development of individualized, client-focused strategies, our team works tirelessly to create a blueprint for success and advance our clients' interests in every matter.
